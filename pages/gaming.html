Gaming

Everyone knows about gaming already, of course, and it is no surprise when you find out that it is someone's hobby these days. I have been interested in gaming from a young age when I was first introduced to the wonderous world of Minecraft. Since then I have explored other titles and genres, with loads of laughs with friends sprinkled everywhere.

My personal preference of genre would be survival and open-world games, like:

  • Minecraft
  • Terraria
  • Skyrim
  • The Witcher 3
  • Deep Rock Galactic
I also enjoy complex games that have a high skill ceiling, such as you would find in real-time-strategy (RTS) games like Age of Empires.

There are those that see games as an unhealthy activity and a waste of time, but from where I come from they are just way of relieving stress and reconnecting with friends, and even connecting with complete strangers. I see them as no less unhealthy or different from smoking to relieve stress or going to parties to reconnect with friends. Take it as the introvert's alternative.
