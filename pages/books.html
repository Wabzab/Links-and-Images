Favourite Books

I have read a lot of books over the years and my favourites do often change. However, I consistently enjoy the fantasy genre more than any other.

My current favourites include:

  • Number9Dream - David Mitchell
  • The Tower of Fools - Andrzej Sapkowski
  • The Witcher Series - Andrzej Sapkowski
  • Circe - Madeline Miller
  • The Three-Body Problem - Liu Cixin
